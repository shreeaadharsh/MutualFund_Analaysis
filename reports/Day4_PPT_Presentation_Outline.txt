--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 4: Sharpe & Sortino Ratios (Risk-Adjusted Returns)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: Sharpe & Sortino Ratios
- Details:
  * Used RBI repo rate proxy of 6.5% as the Risk-Free Rate.
  * Sharpe Ratio evaluates return per unit of total risk.
  * Sortino Ratio evaluates return per unit of downside risk (ignoring positive volatility).
  * Outperformance seen in Large Cap and Flexicap schemes.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 5: Alpha & Beta (OLS Regression)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: Market Sensitivity & Alpha Generation
- Details:
  * Aligned fund daily returns with NIFTY 100 returns.
  * Run OLS linear regression: Beta is the slope, Alpha is the annualized intercept.
  * Direct plans display a significant +1.5% to 2.5% annualized Alpha advantage over regular plans solely due to lower expense ratios.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 6: Maximum Drawdowns & Recovery
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: Risk & Drawdown Recovery Analysis
- Details:
  * Max Drawdown tracks peak-to-trough decline.
  * Worst drawdown date range identified for all 40 schemes (most occurred during the mid-2024 correction).
  * Tracked peak dates, trough dates, and recovery dates (noting which funds recovered quickest).

SLIDE 7: Composite Fund Scorecard Model
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: Fund Scorecard Rankings
- Visual: Snapshot of fund_scorecard.csv
- Details:
