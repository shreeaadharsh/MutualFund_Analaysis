SLIDE 9: Folio Expansion & Industry Milestones
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: Growth of Mutual Fund Folios
- Visual: Multi-line chart tracking total, equity, and debt folios (folio_count_growth.png)
- Highlights:
  * Total folios grew from 13.26 Crore (Jan 2022) to 26.12 Crore (Dec 2025).
  * Crossed the major milestone of 20 Crore folios in March 2024.
  * Folio growth is driven 100% by equity mutual funds; debt folios remained flat.

SLIDE 10: Portfolio Sector Weighting & Return Correlation Matrix
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: Asset Allocation & Return Co-Movements
- Visuals: Sector Allocation Donut, Correlation Matrix Heatmap (sector_allocation_donut.png, nav_returns_correlation.png)
- Highlights:
