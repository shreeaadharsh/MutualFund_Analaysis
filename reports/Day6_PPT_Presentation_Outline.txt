SLIDE 2: Advanced Risk Modeling (VaR & CVaR)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: Value at Risk (VaR) & Conditional VaR (CVaR)
- Details:
  * Computed historical 95% VaR representing the maximum daily loss at a 95% confidence level.
  * Computed CVaR representing average loss on tail days exceeding the VaR boundary.
  * Small Cap funds display higher tail loss expectations (~2.2% daily VaR) than Large Cap products (~1.3% daily VaR).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 3: Rolling Sharpe Ratio Cycles
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: Rolling 90-Day Sharpe Ratio Dynamics
- Visual: rolling_sharpe_chart.png
- Details:
  * Plotted rolling risk-adjusted returns for 5 key Large Cap benchmark funds.
  * Risk efficiency reached historical highs above 3.5 in the 2023 rally, but retreated below 0.5 in early 2024.
  * Details how market cycles drive short-term fund efficiency.

SLIDE 4: Retail Investor Cohort Analytics
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: Behavioral Analysis of Investor Cohorts
- Details:
  * Segmented database by first transaction year (2024 and 2025 cohorts).
  * 2024 Cohort: Represented massive retail participation with over ₹349 Crore invested across 4,803 unique accounts.
  * 2025 Cohort: Average ticket size increased to ₹13,505 showing larger starting capital inputs.
  * Top scheme preference for the 2024 cohort was UTI Nifty 50 Index Fund.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 5: SIP Continuity & Retention Management
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: SIP Continuity Analysis & Default Flagging
- Details:
  * Filtered database for investors with 6 or more monthly transactions.
  * Tracked date gaps between consecutive payments.
  * Flagged accounts with average intervals exceeding 35 days as "At-Risk" of churn.
  * Provides target listings for active default correction programs.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 6: Portfolio Concentration (Sector HHI)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Heading: Herfindahl-Hirschman Index (HHI) for Sector Concentration
- Details:
  * Sector HHI computed by summing squared percentage sector allocations.
  * High HHI (>2500) indicates concentrated thematic allocations.
  * Axis Bluechip Regular led the concentration rankings (HHI of 2,967) with heavy allocations to Banking and IT.
  * Provides structural risk profiling for active portfolio management.
